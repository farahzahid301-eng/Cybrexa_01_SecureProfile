About Me

Hello! I am a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) student at the University of Management and Technology (UMT), Lahore. My academic journey has driven me deep into the world of computer science, where I discovered my true passion: Cybersecurity and Ethical Hacking.

I don't just write code; I focus on how to secure it. From understanding Linux fundamentals and configuring secure networks to practicing vulnerability assessments with tools like Nmap and Metasploit, I actively translate theoretical knowledge into hands-on lab practice.
